qualis

Data quality that tells you what failed — not just that something did.

How this works

  1. Upload a CSV. It stays on your machine.
  2. See the profile and rules qualis suggests, each with evidence.
  3. Review — accept, send back, or reject every suggestion.
  4. Run the checks and read your data-quality score.
  5. Export a rulebook you can keep using from the CLI.

CSV upload arrives in the next step of the build. This is the landing screen — the server is running locally on your machine.

Running locally on 127.0.0.1. Your data never leaves this machine.
